Chengkai Liu (刘成锴)

I am a fourth-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Texas A&M University (TAMU), advised by Prof. James Caverlee.

I received my bachelor’s degree from ACM Honors class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2022, advised by Prof. Yong Yu.

My research interests include efficient recommender systems, large language models, and generative models. In Summer 2025, I interned at Amazon Prime Video, working on multimodal LLMs and personalized recommendation.
