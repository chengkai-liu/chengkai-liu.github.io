Chengkai Liu (刘成锴)

I am a third-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Texas A&M University (TAMU), advised by Prof. James Caverlee.

I received my bachelor’s degree from ACM Honors class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in 2022, advised by Prof. Yong Yu.
